Our Story

More About Us

At For Legacy, we are dedicated to acquiring and nurturing well-established businesses, particularly from owners who are looking to retire. We understand the emotional and financial significance of these businesses, and our mission is to continue their legacy by maintaining their core values, brand identity, and long-term success.

  • Stewardship over Ownership: We don't just acquire businesses, we take on the responsibility of preserving their legacy. Our focus is on safeguarding the values and brands built over generations.
  • Long-Term Investment: Our approach is to hold businesses for the long run—over decade's ensuring stability, sustained growth, and innovation. We believe in building on success rather than selling off assets.
  • Focused Growth: We carefully expand into new business models only when we have mastered our current focus areas, allowing for controlled, sustainable growth.

With over 36 years of experience in construction and a proven track record in scaling multimillion-pound companies, we combine industry expertise and cutting-edge technology to help businesses continue their growth. At For Legacy, we are committed to building lasting partnerships and ensuring that your hard-earned business thrives well into the future.
