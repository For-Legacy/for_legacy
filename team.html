Why We're the Right Team for Your Business

With decades of experience across diverse industries, our team is uniquely positioned to help your business thrive. Ashton's extensive background with trades like builders and plumbers ensures we understand the importance of delivering high-quality service to customers in the home services market. Meanwhile, Daniel's success in scaling technology companies brings a modern touch, integrating innovation and digital marketing. Together, we respect your legacy while ushering in the future of your business.

Daniel's background in scaling tech-driven businesses provides a unique advantage for legacy businesses looking to modernize while maintaining their core values. His experience leading product and engineering teams across industries like education, cloud services, and financial platforms means he understands how to integrate technology in a way that enhances operations rather than disrupts them. Whether it's streamlining processes, improving customer experience, or leveraging data for smarter decision-making, Daniel's expertise ensures that businesses can evolve while staying true to their original mission. His ability to drive growth while fostering stability makes him a valuable partner for owners seeking a future-proof transition.

Ashton's career spans leadership roles across architectural, surveying, and construction firms, giving him a firsthand understanding of what it takes to build and sustain a successful business in the trades. As Director of Ashton Paul Limited and former Architectural Director at Kingsley Symes & Scott, he has worked on everything from residential property developments to large-scale commercial projects, always prioritizing quality, trust, and customer satisfaction. His ability to guide businesses through complex transitions while preserving their core values makes him a trusted partner for business owners looking to retire with confidence. At For Legacy, Ashton applies his decades of experience to ensure that businesses in the trades and home services continue to thrive for generations to come.
